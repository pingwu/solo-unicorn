# Goals & Objectives

This folder is dedicated to setting, tracking, and reviewing your personal and professional goals.

## Purpose:
- Define clear, actionable goals across various aspects of your life.
- Monitor progress and make adjustments as needed.
- Maintain focus and motivation towards achieving your aspirations.

## Suggested Content:
- Annual, quarterly, or project-specific goal documents.
- SMART goal definitions (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound).
- Action plans, milestones, and progress logs.
- Reflections on goal attainment and lessons learned.

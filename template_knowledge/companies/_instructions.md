# Companies Knowledge

This folder is dedicated to information about companies you are interested in, have worked with, or are researching.

## Purpose:
- Track potential employers or clients.
- Store research on market trends, competitors, and industry leaders.
- Keep notes on company culture, values, and important contacts.

## Suggested Content:
- Company profiles (e.g., mission, vision, products/services).
- Interview notes and application details.
- Articles, reports, or news related to the company.
- Networking contacts and their roles.

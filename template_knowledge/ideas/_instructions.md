# Ideas & Brainstorms

This folder is a repository for all your ideas, big or small, and brainstorming sessions.

## Purpose:
- Capture nascent ideas before they are fully formed.
- Document brainstorming sessions for projects, problem-solving, or creative endeavors.
- Explore concepts and connections freely.

## Suggested Content:
- Markdown files for individual ideas or projects.
- Mind maps, flowcharts (if supported by Obsidian plugins).
- Lists of related thoughts, questions, and potential actions.

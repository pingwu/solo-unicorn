# ANCHOR.md — Strategic Anchor

**Purpose:** Help specialists ship end-to-end — use AI to fill the gaps between their expertise and everything else it takes to go from requirement to production.

**Who we serve:** Engineers, PMs, and technical specialists who are strong in their domain but have never carried an application from requirement to production alone.

**Architectural commitments:**
- Stay agent-agnostic. The same skills, instructions, and knowledge run through any CLI (Claude, Gemini, others) — no vendor lock-in.
- Keep context as the user's codebase. Knowledge lives as local markdown in `../my_knowledge/`, not a vendor's servers.
- Mirror production from day one. Container-first is non-negotiable (rules in [UNICORN_CONSTITUTION.md](UNICORN_CONSTITUTION.md)).
- Ship MVPs, test-first. Prove the concept; iterate. Every session produces portfolio-ready work.
- Operate as an Office. Each role (CTO, CPO, CMO…) maps to skills in `skills/`; act as Chief of Staff — keep the user on one achievable goal.

**North-star constraint:** Fill the gaps, never replace the user's expertise or the human loop — Ask your Why, Listen to your audience, Do with impact.
